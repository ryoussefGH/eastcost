Our Story

East Coast Arsenal is on 191 in Lower Nazareth! We have brought a new standard in firearm sales, service, and support. Whether you're purchasing your very first firearm, expanding a well-curated collection, or looking for trusted FFL/NFA services, we're your go-to destination.

Our modern, fully licensed facility is open to the public. We have designed every square foot to deliver a clean, secure, and professional experience backed by real-world knowledge. Whether you're a weekend enthusiast, a seasoned collector, or a professional in the field, East Coast Arsenal is the place where serious firearm owners come to do business. Come see us.

BUILT ON EXCELLENCE

East Coast Arsenal represents a new standard in firearms retail for the Lehigh Valley. We're not just another gun shop—we're a fully licensed, modern firearms establishment dedicated to serving our community with integrity, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to responsible gun ownership.

From the first-time buyer seeking guidance to the seasoned collector expanding their arsenal, we provide a welcoming environment where knowledge meets professionalism. Our team brings decades of combined experience to ensure every customer receives personalized attention and expert advice.

YOUR TRUSTED PARTNER

Whether you're purchasing your first firearm, adding to your collection, or in need of specialized FFL and NFA services, East Coast Arsenal is your trusted partner. We pride ourselves on creating a safe, comfortable, and educational environment where every question is welcome and every customer is valued.

Our commitment extends beyond sales—we're here to support law enforcement, first responders, and responsible gun owners throughout their ownership journey.

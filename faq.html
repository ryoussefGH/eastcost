Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to purchase a firearm?

For handguns, you must be a Pennsylvania resident 21 years or older with a valid state-issued ID. For long guns (rifles/shotguns), you must be 18 years or older. All firearm purchases require passing the Pennsylvania Instant Check System (PICS) background check at our store.

What is an FFL Transfer?

An FFL (Federal Firearms License) transfer is required when you purchase a firearm online or from an out-of-state seller. The seller ships the firearm to a local FFL dealer (like us), and we complete the required background check and paperwork for you to legally take possession of it.

Do you buy used guns?

Yes! We buy, sell, and trade used firearms. Bring your unloaded and cased firearm to the store, and we can provide you with a cash quote or trade-in value towards a new purchase.

Shipping & Return Policy

PURCHASING FIREARMS ONLINE

It is illegal to ship firearms to anyone not holding an active Federal Firearms License (FFL). For that reason, firearms purchased online may be picked up at our store, or shipped to an FFL (gun store) near you. We may contact and verify the FFL you select during checkout before we ship your firearm(s). The buyer is encouraged to contact the receiving FFL before purchase.

We need a copy of the FFL license for the dealer of your choice. If we do not have a copy on file, we will need to receive one. East Coast Arsenal cannot be blamed for dealers dragging their feet and delaying sending us their information.

PURCHASING NFA ITEMS ONLINE

Shipping and processing times depend on what you order and/or how fast your dealer contacts us. If you order a silencer / suppressor your dealer must send us their information before East Coast Arsenal can start the form 3 dealer transfer. The form 3 dealer transfer typically takes no more than 2 weeks. So if you order a silencer / suppressor it would be expected to ship no more than 2 weeks AFTER the dealer it is shipping to contacts East Coast Arsenal. East Coast Arsenal cannot be blamed for dealers dragging their feet and delaying sending us their information.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in the shipment of your order, East Coast Arsenal will contact you by email.

SHIPPING RATES & DELIVERY RATES

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. Delivery delays can occasionally occur.

SHIPMENT CONFIRMATION

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
